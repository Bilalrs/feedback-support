Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Information We Collect

  • Profile Data: name, gender, birthday, and phone number (optional; you choose what to share).
  • Location Updates: your device periodically uploads its GPS location to Apple’s CloudKit service (encrypted in transit).
  • Anonymous Encounter Data: our AWS Lightsail server reads those CloudKit location records, computes which users were within 15 ft of each other for at least 5 minutes (then apart for 20 minutes), and logs those encounters for processing.
  • Feedback Content: your 1–5 ratings and freeform comments after an encounter.
  • Sign in with Apple: we only receive an opaque Apple ID identifier; we do not collect your email or password.

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you via Sign in with Apple.
  • To store and sync your profile data via CloudKit.
  • To detect and record encounters on our AWS server based on those CloudKit location updates.
  • To send you a push notification prompting feedback after each encounter.
  • To compile and deliver weekly feedback every Wednesday at 5 PM.

Sharing & Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Profile data and location updates are stored encrypted at rest in CloudKit; encounter logs and feedback are stored encrypted on our AWS Lightsail servers. We do not share your personal details or feedback with any third parties.

Your Choices

  • You can edit or delete your profile data (name, gender, birthday, phone) anytime in Edit Profile.
  • To opt out of encounter detection, disable Location Services for this app in your device settings.
