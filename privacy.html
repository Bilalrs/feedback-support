Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Information We Collect

  • Profile Data: name, gender, birthday, and phone number (optional; you choose what to share).
  • Anonymous Encounter Data: proximity timestamps when you’re within 15 ft of another user for the required duration. These timestamps are collected on-device, then transmitted securely (TLS) to our AWS Lightsail server for processing and storage.
  • Feedback Content: your 1–5 ratings and freeform comments.
  • Sign in with Apple: we only receive an opaque Apple identifier; no email or password is stored by us.

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you via Sign in with Apple.
  • To detect encounters (15 ft proximity + 5 min dwell + 20 min drift) on our server and trigger notifications.
  • To power the Radar view, Feedback Breakdown, and weekly digest.
  • To send push notifications prompting feedback within 20 min of an encounter.

Sharing & Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Encounter data and feedback are stored encrypted at rest on our AWS Lightsail servers. We do not share your personal details or feedback outside the app.

Your Choices

  • You can edit or delete your profile (name, gender, birthday, phone) at any time in Settings.
  • You can delete individual feedback items or your entire feedback history via the “Clear History” option.
  • To opt out of encounter detection, disable Location Services for this app in your device settings.
